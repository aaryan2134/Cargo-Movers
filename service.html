Our Service Information

Explore the range of services offered by Cargo Movers, designed to meet all your logistics requirements.

Air Freight Services

Cargo Movers specializes in air freight, offering competitive rates and guaranteed delivery to major destinations. Our services include same-day AWB, pickup from exporter factory, fast custom clearance, and constant shipment tracking. We have counterparts at major global destinations and provide optional custom clearance at destination.

Custom Clearance Services

Cargo Movers offers efficient custom clearance services to ensure timely delivery of documents and parcels globally. We provide services for DEPB, DGFT, ISO Certificates, Advance License, EPCG License, and more. Our team is well-versed in the latest regulations and processes to expedite your shipments.

International Courier

Fast Track International, a division of Cargo Movers, provides air express services for transporting documents and samples globally. With a well-devised tracking system, we ensure the safety and security of your shipments. We operate through networks in the USA, UK, Singapore, and Germany, offering reliable services with DHL, UPS, and others.

Warehousing Services

Cargo Movers offers strategically located warehousing services with modern facilities and equipment. We provide reliable and prompt services that have been appreciated by our clients. Our warehouses are equipped to handle a variety of goods with efficiency and care.

Door to Door Delivery

Our door-to-door delivery service covers import shipments at key locations and provides reliable transportation to various destinations. We specialize in delivery to Central Asian Republics and other landlocked regions via multimodal transport solutions.
