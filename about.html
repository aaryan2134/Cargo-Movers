About Us

About Our Business

Cargo Movers, a renowned NVOCC with its own MTO and IATA certification, has been serving the logistics industry for over a decade. We are your one-stop solution for all logistics needs, offering comprehensive services such as Freight Forwarding, Import & Exports, Documentation, Customs Clearance, and Local Transportation. Our expertise spans across various segments including General Cargo, Project Cargo, Exhibition Cargo, Carnet, Third Country Export, and Transfer of Residence (TR). We specialize in handling Customs Procedures & Processing under different License Schemes like Advance License and EPCG.


As an IATA Certified Company, Cargo Movers boasts a team of well-qualified DGR certified staff. We are particularly specialized in SEA SHIPMENTS from INDIA to CIS COUNTRIES and AFGHANISTAN via BANDARABBAS and KARACHI.


In addition to our logistics services, we offer export/import consultancy for DEPB, DBK, export incentives, DGFT documentation, brand rate fixation for drawback, advance license, EPCG, 100% EOU, export house status, and central excise services. We also provide consultancy, implementation, and training for ISO 9001, ensuring high standards in all aspects of our operations. Trust Cargo Movers for all your logistics needs.
