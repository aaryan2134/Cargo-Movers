Mr. JK Arora

CEO & Founder

P.G.D.I.T

India Institute of Foreign Trade

We are one of the leading customs clearing agents in the customs brokerage industry in India, established in 2003 by J.K. Arora, a seasoned logistics and customs professional with a 25-year track record of optimizing operations and reducing costs. Our expertise lies in the clearance of industrial, medical, and surgical goods, and we are the authorized exclusive Customs House Agent for many top importers in India. We provide a complete range of services related to imports & exports. On average, we clear lakhs of quintals of goods, containing more than 8000 packages valued at approximately Rs. 100 crores annually. We are one of the few CHAs in India equipped with the RES facility by the customs department since 2003. This allows us to file all Bills of Entry/Shipping Bills online, with processed document prints obtained in our office. We guarantee damurrage-free clearance within a stipulated 72-hour period after landing, provided all necessary documents and government dues are submitted in advance, and no time-consuming objections are raised by customs or other agencies. We have extensive experience in sea shipments to CIS countries and Afghanistan via Bandar Abbas and Karachi. Additionally, we offer consultancy and implementation services for ISO 9001.
