We are one of the leading Customs Clearing Agents in customs brokerage industry in India established in 2006 by Mr. Yashpal Anand (and currently being handled jointly with his son Mr. Neeraj Anand). Our expertise lies in clearance of Industrial, Medical and Surgical goods & are authorized exclusive Customs House Agent for many of top Importers in India. We have been hosting a healthy clientele consistently with us to whom, we are providing complete range of Services related to imports & exports and are clearing on an average Lakhs quintals of goods containing more the 8000 packages valued at approx Rs. 100 crores annually. We are one of the CHA’s in India to be equipped with RES facility by the customs department W.e.f 2006, and hence we use the online facility to file all Bills of Entry/Shipping Bills in our office and processed documents prints are obtained in our office only due to RES facility provided by the customs. We facilitate import & export consolidation, freight forwarding and all imports & export goods are brought under consolidation. We guarantee damurrage free clearance within stipulated 72 hours period after landing. For this, all the necessary documents and Govt dues must be provided in DIAL in advance and there should be no(unlikely) time consuming objection raised by customs, dial in ADC, or any other agency. Excellent rapport with Airlines, Customs, IAAI, CWC is an added feather in our cap. We have a dedicated workforce committed to excel in providing the services to the customers and meeting the customer specific demands. We have a Pre-Deposit account at the airport to cater to the needs of the warehousing/ handling charges on behalf of our clients for speedy clearance of the shipments. We also arrange delivery vans for speedy transportation of your valuable cargo to & fro airport and ICDs in Delhi. We specialize in clearance of perishable & refrigerated import cargo and we have specialized dedicated staff to handle & clear such goods expeditiously.
